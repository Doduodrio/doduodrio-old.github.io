Corn Maze: An A-maize-ing Autumn Adventure!

This game tries to emulate the feeling of playing an old retro game, featuring a three-color palette (used on this website) and simple pixel art. You are a farmer at the corn maze one fine autumn day, and your goal is to find the end of the maze! Along the way, you can talk to various NPCs with entertaining (I hope) dialogue. I chose to use Scratch because it's quite easy to use, and you can do quite a lot with it.

Rainbow Quest


A text-based game centered around battling enemies! These enemies are located in rooms named after colors of the rainbow. I feel like the gameplay is a little dry and repetitve, but I'm really proud of it because the battle system took me a long time to make. (Just look at how many lines the battle function is!) Hope you enjoy! (Note: If you don't see the text carat, that's because I hid it. It looks better that way.)
